Localization support is coming soon!

 

The community has many users whose primary language is not English and we want to make sure our applications are accessible to everyone!

With the UGX Launcher & UGXL Installer it's possible to translate any text into another language.

 

If you want to see your native language implemented into our applicatinos, check out the Community Contributions. If you would like to help us translate our apps into your native language, please look at Translate into new language.
