The UGX Launcher will require Authentication with your  www.ugx-mods.com (forum) account.

   

 

We decided this for multiple reasons:

  • Prevent download spam.
  • More personalized features for the user, both now and in the future with more additions.
  • Better control & moderating from our end.
  • Access control based on the user for testing and also restriction purposes.

The authentication over the internet will be transmitted over an encrypted connection. [1]

Everyone can access the UGX Launcher as long as they aren't banned / blocked from www.ugx-mods.com

 

 

(1) This product includes software developed by the OpenSSL Project for use in the OpenSSL Toolkit. (http://www.openssl.org/)
