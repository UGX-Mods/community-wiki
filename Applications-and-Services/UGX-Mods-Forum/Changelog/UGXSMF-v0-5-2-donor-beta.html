Summary

Thanks to all for your great feedback ❤️ . This release contains better notifications and minor improvements + some bug fixes.

Important highlights from this release

  • Major improvements to notifications (alerts)
    • Restyle of notifications (working great on mobile now as well)
    • Upgrade of alerts plugin, including HTML 5 / Browser notifications
    • Notifications won't appear for unapproved posts now
    • Notifications will be removed for deleted posts / topics"
  • Some more pages received a facelift (like notifications) with some usability changes.

    • For example, the notification pages:


      Before (old)

      After (new facelift)

  • After login will redirect to previous page now.
  • Internal UGX-Mods links won't show a redirect to external page now
  • Jump to Dropdown received a nice facelift
  • Development and Moderation
    • A lot of time was spent to ensure a better quality in the future with End-2-End tests.
    • Also some great improvements for Moderators
    • UGX-Mods staff can write notifications to certain groups with complex content

Known issues

  • Supported Browsers / Devices
    • Currently only Firefox & Chrome has been heavily test.
    • Internet Explorer 11 & Edge has been partially tested.
    • There was no extensive testing on mobile devices yet
    • We need your feedback if something isn't working on your device
  • There are (apparently) still some annoying issues with CKEditor. We spent a lot of time to improve it, so keep reporting issues!

Whats next?

We await (your) community feedback now ❤️ While working on additional improvements and features, the feedback from the community will be put together into more frequent updates.

The next update (v0.5.3-donor-beta) will improve the PM display, some internal development preparations and fix some quirks with CKEditor.

It has been a busy year and there will be soon progress on other Applications & Services again - stay tuned!
