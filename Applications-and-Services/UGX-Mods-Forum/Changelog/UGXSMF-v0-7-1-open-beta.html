Summary

Minor release with bug fixes and improvements. Donors can choose now different color scheme of the site. (red, yellow, green, orange, purple, cyan, black)

Important highlights from this release

  • Added color schemes for donors
    • Donors can choose now between a few color schemes, including red, yellow, purple, black, ...
  • Email bounces are now notified to the user
    • these happens when your email is invalid (deleted, etc) or when your inbox is full / not available.
  • Improvements
    • mobile navigation performance optimization (using hardware acceleration now)
    • On registration error all form values are remembered and restored
    • We don't store indefinitely mail events anymore and also store less (personal) data.
  • Bugfixes
    • display all grand-child boards now
    • fix mobile navigation opens itself on scroll down left
    • registration works in all browsers again
