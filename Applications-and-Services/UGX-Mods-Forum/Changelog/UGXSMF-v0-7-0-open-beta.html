Summary

The UGX-Mods Forum Beta is now open to everyone! What are you waiting for?! Visit  https://beta.ugx-mods.com  and give us some great feedback 

We also implemented technical solutions for the EU's  General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Important highlights from this release

  • We are ready for GDPR -  sort of
    • Frist of all - everyone will benefit from these improvements and changes!
    • We put a lot of effort and work in a short amount of time to do the (what we believe) right steps
    • We offer you a new Privacy Settings page, available here:  https://beta.ugx-mods.com/forum/privacy
    • In your Profile under Privacy, a new Export Tool was developed
      • you can now export your personal data (even your forum messages!)
    • We reviewed the Forum code and on account deletion we will anonymize forum users now.
      • Once your account deletion was removed, no one can identify you anymore
    • We reworked our Privacy Policy  which will be available on May 25th 2018.
    • We anonymize your IP for Google Analytics tracking now and won't track you before you haven't given your consent. (more about this on our  Privacy Policy )
  • New Development Roadmap - we want to show more information on what we are working 
    • head over to  https://beta.ugx-mods.com/ roadmap  and get insight into upcoming updates.
    • This is a representation of our JIRA Version list which is unfortunately no longer available to the public
  • Manage your newsletter subscriptions (Account > Newsletter)
    • this is incomplete and only visually working but not functional yet!
    • We will announce it once it's ready in an upcoming update
  • The UGX Blog code has been improved and the whole system has been refined a bit.
  • Usability improvements
  • Bugfixes
    • the new introduced mobile menu has been refined and the popout bug should be gone
