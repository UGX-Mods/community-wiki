About

The avatar editor allows to modify and upload an image which will be set as your UGX-Mods Forum avatar

Navigate to your Forum Profile Settings to get started.

Change avatar

Select Upload an avatar and click on the avatar to your right. Alternatively you can directly drag and drop a file onto it or click on the Choose avatar file button.
You can also launch die Avatar Editor directly by clicking on the Avatar Editor button and choose / modify the image.

Crop avatar

Inside of the Avatar Editor, you'll land on the Crop mode (if an avatar image was chosen).

From there you can rotate, zoom and crop the image.

You can choose a background color in case if you rotate or move the crop area out of the image to override the default color (black background).

The Free / Restricted mode allows you to crop the image either within the image (Restricted mode) or freely and be able to move it outside of the image.

A double click on the slider thumb control will reset the given modification:

(this example also shows perfectly what the background color does)

Once satisfied, you can Finish or continue with Finetune

Finetune

The Finetune mode allows you to refine your image and apply various image tweaks.

Once satisfied, you can Finish or continue with Filters

Filters

The Filters mode allows you to apply image filters.

If you want to apply multiple ones, be sure to check Apply multiple filters

Once satisfied, you can Finish or continue with Stickers

Stickers

The Stickers (and Shapes) mode allow you to apply emojis, emotes and shapes on your image.

Shapes can be used for example to blackout a certain area.

  • You can place up to 30 items (layers) and order them to your liking.
  • Each layer can be configured and tweaked with the mouse or the settings (settings icon on each layer)
  • If an item is selected, additional controls appear at the bottom of the Layers: duplicate, settings, reset and remove
  • If the area is too small to edit, click on the Expand button and use the zoom slider (or mousewheel / touch zoom).

Once satisfied, you can Finish or go back to another mode and refine the image.
