New registered accounts on UGX-Mods have certain restrictions and are limited in their action:

  • New forum posts or topics need manual approval by an admin / forum moderator
  • You can only have up to two unapproved forum posts / topics
  • Some profile information is hidden (signature, social profile fields, links)


The restriction is lifted if:

  • User reached the forum group "Legless Crawler" (requires 5 approved forum posts / topics)
  • User has the forum group Donator (Make a donation here: https://www.ugx-mods.com/forum/treasury/ )
