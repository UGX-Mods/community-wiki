Zones in BO3 pretty much work the same way they did in World at War, so this page will only cover the changes in BO3. For a full guide to zones, refer to Ultimate Guide to Zones in v1.4 Maps.

 

Creating Zones

Drag a info\volume from the entity browser into your map and give the volume the zone KVPs you learned from the WaW guide.

KVP Changes

Zones now also act as playable area for players. If you want a zone to denote an area that the player is allowed to be, add the following KVP to it:

Key

Value

script_noteworthy

player_volume

 

You'll notice that if you don't have any player volumes in your map, you'll die as soon as you spawn into your map.
