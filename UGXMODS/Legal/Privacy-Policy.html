What information do we collect or receive?

The data we collect, depending on what products & services you use, includes:

  • mandatory account data

  • additional optional account data
    • secret question & answer
  • additional optional profile data

    • full name

Web & Application Log Data

By visiting our products and sites we log like probably everyone else the request which usually contains the following information:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of the request
  • Time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)

  • volume of data transmitted
  • URL of the website from which the request came
