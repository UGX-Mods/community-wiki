In February 2018 we introduced HTML emails for the UGX-Mods Forum .

We don't have the budget to test our email templates on any device / e-mail client, therefore we need your feedback .

Required Information

We need to know the following information if you want to report an template issue:

  1. What email did you received
  2. On what operating system did you open it? (computer, android, ios, ..)
  3. What mail client do you use? (Outlook, Gmail, Thunderbird, ....)
  4. A description about the issue
  5. (Optional) A screenshot of the issue

Otherwise simply let us know what we can do for you.
