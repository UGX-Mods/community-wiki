Difference between account and username

The username (also known as account name) is the name you used during the registration process and is used to login. (as alternative to your email)

The name (also known as display name) is used as name which is visible to all other forum users. (only you and admins can see your account name)


Change (display) name

  1. Visit your account profile (https://www.ugx-mods.com/forum/profile/area=account)
  2. Change your Name (username / display name)
    1. This change can be done every 14 days


Change account name

  1. Contact us through our UGX Help Center.
    1. Request an account change name
  2. You will receive confirmation from our support and an password reset email.
  3. Reset your password (you can use your old one if you don't want to change it)
