Summary

This release contains external application connections (Social Integrations) for major providers. These allow to create a new account faster on UGX-Mods and also to login with it.
Everyone can link their accounts with UGX-Mods in order to login with them in the future.

Users who link their Discord Account and are on our UGX-Mods Discord Server will receive automatically their correct Discord Groups.

Important highlights from this release

  • Connections Profile Page:
    manage your connections, link and unlink your accounts.

  • Faster registration with social connections:
    Register with Steam, Discord, Twitch, Google, Yahoo, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn
  • Password-less login with social connections:
    After registration with an social application or after connecting one application with your existing UGX-Mods account it is possible to login with it.
  • Connection with Discord will sync your Discord Groups automatically:
    After connection all supported Discord roles will be assigned based on your Forum Groups. Automatically and whenever they change 
    (it is changed usually instant - but sometimes it might take a bit depending how many users will be synced)
  • Many signup / registration fixes and improvements
  • Some style tweaks 
  • Hotfix: added proper IPv6 support to the forum.


Notes about social connections and what UGX-Mods does store:

At the moment we only ready your data to prefill the registration form. If you already have an account and you connect one provider with your UGX-Mods account, only the provider unique identifier is saved in order to identify you in the future (so that you can login for example with your Twitter account) [ we don't store any application sessions or tokens! ].
We don't write to your account or do anything on behalf of you (like writing a tweet on Twitter and such). - IF we are going to do this in the future, you have to re authenticate and agree to this!
Certain providers also have the option that you can manually define what we can read (and therefore use). For the registration we fetch usually the following information: email (required to sent you the confirm email), username, personal name, location, personal text (for signature). - During the registration it's possible to change or clear the pre filled data.

TL;DR We can't do anything critical with your data and use it only to prefill the registration form and identify so that you can login.
