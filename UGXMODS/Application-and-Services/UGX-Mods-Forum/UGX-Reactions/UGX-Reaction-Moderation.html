Topic Moderation

You can freeze topic reactions (can't add or remove any more reactions) or remove all reactions of this topic.

The remove all will ask for confirmation first. The freeze topic will link to the ban page with prefilled information:


Bans / Freezes

You can issue a ban by visiting an users profile:


Alternatively go to the ban page (Main navigation: Moderate > UGX Reactions - Then in the Moderation Navigation on UGX Reactions > Bans / Freezes):

Here you can view current bans and issue new ones. From there you can also issue freezes.


Other Moderation Tools

Activity

You can view the activity of reactions.

Stats

You can view certain stats about top users, topics, messages and reactions to get insight and identify potential abuse.

Moderation Logs

Review what other moderators did and to find maybe patterns from the past.
