We would like to make everyone aware of a potential userdata leak.

To answer the critical questions:

 

Q: Should I change my password(s) ?

A: Yes! You should! We highly advise regardless of the threat level to change at least your UGX-Mods Account password.

 

Q: How bad is it? Any known cases?

A: We do not expect that any malicious activity will occur as a result of this breach but we cannot guarantee this - it is out of our control. We regret if you experience any issues from this breach and we hope to regain your trust as best as we can. We have not had any reports of people who have had their information compromised, and to our knowledge no information has actually been released.

 

What happened?

On January 21st 2016 our former team member Michael 'AoKMiKeY' Hillcox dumped the whole development database with over 192k user records. The development database is a separate copy of our real forum database which we use on a separate copy of the forum for development and testing purposes. This database is as recent as November 8th 2015, with 191,980 members in it. He threatened to post it publicly but to our knowledge he has not done so.

Whats next?

  • Our development database will be cleaned up (removal of sensitive, non development-related userdata)
  • We continue to be transparent in touch with you
