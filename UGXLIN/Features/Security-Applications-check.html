The UGX Launcher checks for running Antivirus / Anti-Spyware and Firewalls.

 

* We check for these applications to ensure the best experience for you. Sometimes they create compatibility issues.*

 

 

In the past we had many users who reported issues.

To help you prevent these issues, we check for such applications and if we know any issues with them,

we will link you to the help section to fix the issues fast.

 

Please note, we therefore are required to check the application name and type on our server.

We will collect and store this data anonymously and without any connections to you.

For more information, check out our Privacy Policy in the Legal section.
